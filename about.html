Bangtan Sonyeondan, or Beyond the Scene—is a seven-member South Korean boy band that’s become one of the most influential music groups in the world 🌍. They debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment and quickly rose from underground hip-hop roots to global superstardom

🎤 Members of BTS:

  • RM (leader, rapper, lyricist)🐨
  • Jin (vocalist, known as “Worldwide Handsome”)🐹
  • SUGA (rapper, producer)🐱
  • j-hope (rapper, dancer, mood-maker)🐿️
  • Jimin (vocalist, dancer)🐥
  • V (vocalist, actor)🐻🐯
  • Jungkook (main vocalist, youngest)🐰
